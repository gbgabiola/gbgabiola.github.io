About Me

Hi, I'm Genesis and I'm addicted to building products.

I am a web developer and software engineer based in San Fernando, Pampanga, Philippines. My expertise and interest lies in frontend development with frameworks like React, React-Native, and backend development with node.js, relational, non-relational database and python. I also enjoy dabbling in general UI/UX design with a particular goal of understanding the human side of software development. In the words of Jeff Atwood, "Computers are fascinating machines, but they're mostly a reflection of the people using them... you have to study the people behind the software, too."

When I'm not buried in code, I enjoy hanging out with my girlfriend and with my friends also! I believe the best way to treat yourself is going to an adventure, like traveling to the other place, it doesn't matter how far or how near it is. You can follow my adventures here on Instagram!
